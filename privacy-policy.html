Privacy Policy — Wonder World

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Wonder World ("we", "us", "our") operates WhatsApp-based customer communication services for our indoor entertainment and restaurant business in Pakistan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with us via WhatsApp or our website wonderworldpakistan.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you message our WhatsApp business number (+92 334 1333344), we may collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and display name
  • Messages you send us (text, images, voice notes)
  • Booking details you provide (date, party size, preferences)
  • Basic interaction data (timestamps, delivery status)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your queries about packages, bookings, and events
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • Improve our customer service and response quality
  • Share occasional promotions and offers (only with your consent)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • WhatsApp / Meta Platforms (as part of standard WhatsApp Business API)
  • Our internal staff who handle bookings and queries
  • Service providers we use to run our business (e.g., cloud hosting), under strict confidentiality

4. AI Chatbot

Our WhatsApp line is partially operated by an AI assistant ("Alizeh") that helps answer common questions and route queries. Your messages may be processed by AI systems to generate responses. Complex queries are forwarded to our human team.

5. Data Security

Your messages are transmitted over WhatsApp's end-to-end encrypted network. We store conversation records on secure cloud infrastructure and limit access to authorized personnel only.

6. Data Retention

We keep conversation records for up to 12 months for quality and booking reference purposes. You can request deletion of your data at any time by messaging us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Stop receiving messages from us (reply "STOP" or "UNSUBSCRIBE")

8. Children's Privacy

Wonder World is a family-friendly business. Children's information (name, age for bookings) is collected only through parents/guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.
